The blueberries

Tall bushes, sweeter memories

Our heirloom high-bush blueberries were transplanted in 2023 from a certified organic farm in Snohomish County. The bushes are 10–15 years old and tall — no bending down to pick. While we aren't certified organic, we never spray our blueberries with pesticides.

Our name

In honor of Tekla

Tekla Farms pays homage to Brian's grandmother, Tekla Sophia — a Finnish immigrant who homesteaded and farmed in Northeastern Minnesota. Her name carries the same spirit we bring to the land today: hard work, generosity, and roots that run deep.
