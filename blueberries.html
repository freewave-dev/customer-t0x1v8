U-Pick blueberries

Antioxidant-rich, heirloom high-bush berries grown as close to wild as possible — bring the family and fill a bag.

Heirloom high-bush

10–15 year old bushes, no sore backs

Our heirloom high-bush blueberries were transplanted in 2023 from a certified organic farm in Snohomish County, WA. The bushes are anywhere from 10 to 15 years old and tall — so there's no bending down to pick.

While we are not certified organic, we do not spray our blueberries with any pesticides. Our main varieties are Jersey and Rubel — classic, flavorful heirlooms grown as close to wild as we can.
