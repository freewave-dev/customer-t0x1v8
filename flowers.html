Regenerative flower farming

Blooms grown with love

Thousands of flowers grow in our flower field, adjacent to the blueberries. We don't use any pesticides on our flowers and lean into regenerative, pollinator-friendly practices.

Alongside the flowers, we grow herbs like chamomile, lemon balm, anise hyssop, tulsi basil, and calendula — woven into arrangements and grown for the good of the soil and its pollinators.
