When to come

Seasons on the farm

  • U-Pick blueberries: ripe through late summer — August and September are prime picking.
  • Cut flowers: our 2025 season ran February through October; availability varies each year.
  • At the Island Market: fresh bouquets delivered Tuesdays & Fridays in the growing season.
  • Hours shift with the season and the weather — call or check our Instagram before you head up.
See U-Pick details →

Before you arrive

  • Freezer bags for your berries are provided.
  • A $5 picking pass covers adults under 65 & kids over 10.
  • Bushes are tall — comfortable, back-friendly picking.
  • WA state sales tax is added at checkout; prices may change.
